Privacy Policy

ExplorerFocus · Last updated: 30 June 2026

ExplorerFocus is a desktop file explorer for Windows. It runs entirely on your own computer. There is no ExplorerFocus server, and we do not collect, store, transmit or sell your data. This page explains what the app touches and where it stays.

The short version
  • No backend. Everything happens locally on your device.
  • Cloud and server connections go directly from your machine to the provider — never through us.
  • Tokens and passwords are stored on your machine, encrypted with Windows DPAPI.
  • The app only ever accesses the accounts and files you connect.

Data we collect

None on any server — we don't operate one. The app stores its settings, your configured folders/trees, and your saved connections locally, in your Windows user profile. This information never leaves your device except when you ask the app to connect to a service you chose.

Cloud connections (Dropbox, OneDrive, Google Drive)

Connecting a cloud account is optional. When you do:

  • Authentication uses the provider's official OAuth 2 flow (with PKCE). You sign in on the provider's own page; the app never sees your password.
  • The app receives an access/refresh token that grants access to your own account only. The token is stored locally and encrypted with Windows DPAPI; it is never sent to us or any third party.
  • All file operations happen directly between your computer and the provider's API. Your files do not pass through any ExplorerFocus infrastructure.

Permissions requested, and why:

  • Dropbox — account_info.read (show which account is linked), files.metadata.read (browse folders/files), files.content.read (preview/open/download), files.content.write (upload, create folders, rename, move, delete your files).
  • OneDrive (Microsoft Graph) — Files.ReadWrite and offline_access, to browse and manage your own OneDrive files and keep the connection working between sessions.
  • Google Drive — the Drive scope, to browse and manage your own Drive files.

You can disconnect at any time inside the app, and you can revoke access entirely from your provider's account/security settings (Dropbox connected apps, Microsoft account apps, Google account third-party access).

Remote servers (SFTP / FTP / FTPS / WebDAV)

If you add a remote server, its host, username and credentials are stored locally and encrypted with Windows DPAPI. Connections are made directly from your machine to that server. We never see your servers or credentials.

Local files

The app reads and writes the files and folders you choose to work with, on your own device, just like Windows Explorer. Nothing is uploaded anywhere unless you explicitly perform an upload to a destination you connected.

Software updates

The app can check for new versions by fetching a small version.json file hosted on GitHub Pages. This is an ordinary web request and sends no personal data. You can turn automatic checks off in the app's settings.

Licensing & payments

The optional PREMIUM tier is tied to a device using a locally-computed machine ID (a hash derived from your Windows installation and system volume). It is not personally identifying and is used only to bind a licence to a machine. Donations/payments are handled by PayPal; their privacy policy applies to the payment itself. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Website analytics

This website uses a privacy-friendly page-view counter (abacus.jasoncameron.dev) that counts hits only. It sets no tracking cookies and collects no personal data.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your data. We have no servers that collect it. The only parties your data reaches are the services you connect to, directly.

Children

ExplorerFocus is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
